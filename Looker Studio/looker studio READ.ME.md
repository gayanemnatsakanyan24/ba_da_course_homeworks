This folder contains a Looker Studio dashboard built using the Classic Models database. The dashboard provides a clear and structured overview of how the business is performing across key financial and operational metrics.

The purpose of the dashboard is to help users quickly understand the overall health of the business and identify important trends. It visualizes several core performance indicators, including:

### **Revenue Overview**

A set of charts that display total revenue over time, allowing users to track sales performance, detect seasonality, and monitor growth or decline.

### **Profit Analysis**

Visuals that analyze profit levels by product line, region, or time period. These charts help identify where the business is performing strongly and which areas may require improvement.

### **Credit Utilization Rate**

A metric that shows how effectively customers use the credit extended to them. This helps assess financial risk and customer payment behavior.

### **Top-Performing Products**

A ranking or chart that highlights the products generating the highest revenue or profit. This allows quick identification of best-selling items and supports strategic decision-making such as inventory adjustments or promotional focus.

### **Customer or Regional Insights**

Visual segments that show which customers or geographic areas contribute the most to sales and profitability.
